Strategic implications

Where TS Free wins, where it loses.

TS Free wins against all four real free tiers in the matrix — scores range from 8W/4L (BetterSTR, Hostfully) to 12W/1L (Sunver). Four specific features remain losses: host branding, custom domain, referral revenue, and PMS integration on Hostfully's Free tier.

▲ Where TS loses — 4 gaps

Custom domain — Hostfully includes it free; BetterSTR at $9.95/yr

Hostfully Free includes custom domain at $0. BetterSTR offers a subdomain as a paid add-on ($9.95/yr) — not a free inclusion. Sunver and Houseguide don't offer it on Free either. This is a 1-competitor clean loss (Hostfully), not 2.

Hostfully pricing ↗

Referral revenue — 2 competitors pay hosts

BetterSTR gives hosts 4% on Viator bookings. Hostfully includes Referral Revenue on Free. TS has Viator + Welcome Pickups + Tiqets embedded but excludes any rev-share on Free. Pricing decision, not a product gap.

BetterSTR ↗ Hostfully ↗

PMS integration — Hostfully Free has it natively

Hostfully Free syncs to its own PMS + Devices at $0. TS Free has no PMS integration (paid tiers: 25+ PMS native sync). BetterSTR also has auto-sync but only for hosts who already have a PMS.

Hostfully pricing ↗

AI Itinerary + Virtual Concierge on Hostfully Free

Hostfully Free includes AI Itinerary Planner and Virtual Concierge at $0. TS has in-guidebook Q&A AI (different scope — answers questions vs. builds itineraries). Buyers comparing AI see Hostfully as broader.

Hostfully pricing ↗
● Where TS wins — 9 advantages

Unlimited guidebooks, no PMS to start

Sunver = 1, Hostfully = 1, BetterSTR = unlimited but requires PMS to even onboard. TS Free lets any host create guidebooks from day one — no PMS account or sync setup required.

6 local recommendations — beats all four

TS = 6, Sunver = 5 only, BetterSTR has no built-in recs, Hostfully shows sponsored-only. TS wins this row against every real free tier competitor.

Store at 5% — only working store on a free tier

Sunver Free and Hostfully Free have no Store at all. BetterSTR charges 10%+$0.30. TS Free is the only free tier with a live, purchasable store at a single-digit commission.

Email Memo templates on Free

8/month with criteria-based timing, field codes, and deep links into specific topics. None of the four real free tiers offer scheduled email automation at all.

Auto-translation at $0

Sunver, Hostfully, and BetterSTR all gate translations behind paid tiers ($2/language at BetterSTR). TS Free ships Google auto-translate on every guidebook.

In-guidebook AI Q&A — unique on Free

Chatbot trained on the host's own guidebook content, 3 conversations/month. Sunver gates AI to Gold paid. Houseguide has none. BetterSTR none on Free. Hostfully's AI generates itineraries — a different use case entirely.

5-star Review Protection popup

Routes 4-5★ responses to public review sites, low-star to private feedback. BetterSTR has automated review requests only via paid SMS add-on. The other three free tiers don't offer this at all.

SMS rate ~$0.036 — 2.7× cheaper than the market

BetterSTR's pay-as-you-go SMS is $0.10/message — the only competitor that offers SMS on Free at all. TS's rate is ~$0.036/message, available as a separate add-on. Cheaper per message, no bundle required.

Host branding — beats Hostfully specifically

TS Free now ships logo, colors, and font customization. Sunver, Houseguide, and BetterSTR also offer it free — so this is a tie there. But Hostfully gates branding to Pro+, making TS the stronger free-tier product on brand control.

Recommendations

Three calls to make before launch.

Decision 01

Two gaps worth an explicit pre-launch decision.

  • Referral revenue share: TS has all the embed partners (Viator, Welcome Pickups, Tiqets) already in the guidebook. The question is whether to pass any commission to Free hosts. Two competitors do (BetterSTR 4%, Hostfully as a feature). Low implementation cost; high perceived value.
  • Host branding: 3 of 4 competitors offer it free. If this stays gated, the badge-removal upgrade trigger must be treated as primary — that requires deliberate pricing page positioning, not just a feature gate.
  • Custom domain and AI Itinerary — don't delay launch for these. Only Hostfully includes custom domain on Free (BetterSTR charges $9.95/yr for it). AI Itinerary is Hostfully-specific and TS's Q&A AI is a different (and arguably better) use case.
Local recs (6), store, translations, email scheduling, AI Q&A, review popup — these are already stronger than the market. Launch on these, decide on rev-share.
Decision 02

Validate the 200 view cap against real usage data.

  • All four competitors use guidebook-count gates (Sunver: 1, Hostfully: 1) or property-creation gates (BetterSTR: PMS required). TS is the only one using a view cap — a deliberate tradeoff that lets multi-property hosts onto Free.
  • View caps fire at usage success, not at sign-up. The risk: hosts hit 200 views after guests start engaging — exactly the moment they'd recommend TS to other hosts.
  • If P90 of monthly views across existing accounts is under 200, the cap is well-calibrated. If P90 is 400+, the cap punishes the best-engaged segment.
Pull P50/P90/P99 view distribution before committing to 200/mo. This is a data question, not a gut call.
Decision 03

Position around the three things competitors can't match.

  • No PMS required to start: BetterSTR can't create a property without PMS sync. TS Free works for any host from day one — with manual entry and CSV upload as the fallback.
  • Store from day one: Sunver and Hostfully Free have no store at all. BetterSTR's 10%+$0.30 is twice TS's 5%. Only TS lets a host monetize guests for free without upgrading first.
  • Full guest communication stack: email scheduling + AI Q&A + review popup, all on Free. No competitor ships all three at $0.
"Unlimited properties. Host branding. Store from day one." — three things Hostfully Free can't match simultaneously.
Inspiration · features TS doesn't ship

What competitors do that TS Free doesn't.

Features across the 20 tracked competitors that TS currently doesn't offer. Sourced. Not a roadmap — a scan of what the category is doing. Each card: one idea, one source, two sentences.

AI Itinerary Planner + Virtual Concierge

Source: Hostfully Free

AI generates day-by-day plans for guests based on stay dates and interests. Virtual Concierge handles 24/7 questions — different from TS's content-Q&A chatbot, this proactively builds plans.

Digital check-in form + Security deposit + Police form

Source: Sunver Gold

Post-booking workflow: ID capture, integrated security deposit / deposit waiver, EU legal compliance police form. Adjacent to TS's existing Store + Stripe primitive.

WiFi captive portal email capture

Source: BetterSTR ($2/AP)

Guests connect to property WiFi via branded portal that captures email + name. Pairs naturally with TS's existing guest CRM module (currently capped at 10 on Free).

NFC tap-to-topic

Source: BetterSTR

Physical NFC stickers on appliances, door entry, welcome pack. Guest taps phone to go directly to that guidebook topic — same deep-link primitive as TS QR codes on cheaper hardware.

Post-stay automated review request SMS

Source: BetterSTR ($0.10/msg)

Checkout triggers SMS requesting Google/Airbnb review. TS has the 5-star popup in-guide, but no outbound post-stay push — this is a Memo template + 2WM (Beta) combination TS could nearly ship today.

Folio: multi-property branded portfolio

Source: BetterSTR

All guides grouped under one branded landing page at a custom domain. Natural extension for TS's 5+ property clients where unlimited guidebooks already win.

Native iOS + Android branded apps

Source: HolidayHero (from €30/mo) · Enso Boarding Pass

Host's brand in App Store/Play Store. TS PWA is faster + cheaper to deploy — but the "open the [Brand] app" mental model is a recurring sales objection worth tracking.

Smart lock + IoT depth

Source: Operto Guest

Auto-generated lock codes per reservation, thermostat control, noise monitoring, in-app device control. Thin integration layer on TS (surface device control in guidebook) is feasible without owning hardware.

Damage waiver + Security deposit collection

Source: SuiteOp · Happy Guest

Charge guest for damage waiver or hold security deposit via Stripe before lock code releases. Direct host revenue; adjacent to TS's existing Store + Stripe stack.

Online check-in Boarding Pass flow

Source: Duve · Enso Connect

Pre-arrival step-by-step funnel: passport scan, arrival time, access code — delivered as a "boarding pass." Same data as TS's guidebook delivered as a guided workflow rather than a browse-tree.

Sponsored recommendations / Viator native rev-share

Source: Hostfully Free

Host earns commission on booked local experiences without any setup — Hostfully surfaces sponsored options automatically. TS has Viator embed but Free tier excludes the rev-share.

Multi-agent AI architecture

Source: Enso Connect

Separate agents: one routes, one drafts, one escalates, one analyzes sentiment. TS has a single chatbot trained on content — the next-gen pattern is specialist agent teams.
